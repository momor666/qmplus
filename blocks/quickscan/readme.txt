**********************************
*** QuickScan block for Moodle ***
**********************************

This is QuickScan Dyslexia Screening block for Moodle 2.3+, it explains
student the nature of the test and redirects user to Pico web service to perform
the test. You need to register with Pico to enable QuickScan service for your
organisation.

Installation:

Copy quickscan directory inside /blocks directory of your Moodle, then go to Site
Administration notification page and proceed with installation.
